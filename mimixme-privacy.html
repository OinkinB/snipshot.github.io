Privacy Policy Overview

MimixMe is an AI-powered voice cloning application designed for entertainment purposes only. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you use our app.

The Short Version: We process voice recordings temporarily to create AI-generated voice clips. Voice profiles are stored locally on your device. When generating clips, audio is sent to our secure backend for AI processing, then immediately deleted. We don't share your data with advertisers or third parties for marketing.

Information We Collect

Voice Data You Provide

  • Voice Recordings: Audio samples you record or upload to create voice profiles (10 seconds maximum)
  • Reference Audio: Audio files stored locally on your device for voice cloning
  • Profile Information: Voice names and optional profile pictures you assign to each voice
  • Generated Clips: AI-generated audio clips created using the app, stored in your library

Data Processed During Generation

AI Voice Cloning Process: When you generate a voice clip, we temporarily send your reference audio and text prompt to our secure backend server running Qwen3-TTS AI model. The audio is processed, the clip is generated, and all data is immediately deleted from our servers.

Technical Data

  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version, app version
  • Usage Statistics: Number of generations used (to track free trial limit)
  • Crash Reports: Automatically collected crash logs to improve app stability

How We Use AI Technology

Voice Cloning AI

MimixMe uses Qwen3-TTS, a state-of-the-art text-to-speech AI model, to clone voices:

  • Your reference audio is analyzed to extract voice characteristics
  • The AI generates new speech in the cloned voice based on your text input
  • Processing happens on our secure backend servers in Google Cloud
  • Generated audio is returned to your device within 5-10 seconds
  • All processing data is deleted immediately after generation

Important: AI Limitations & Disclosure

  • AI-Generated Content: All voice clips are clearly AI-generated and synthesized
  • Not Perfect Clones: Voice similarity varies based on audio quality and voice characteristics
  • Entertainment Only: This technology is for fun, comedy, and entertainment - not for impersonation or fraud
  • Audio Quality: Better source audio produces better results

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage (On Your Device)

  • Voice Profiles: Stored permanently in your device's local file system
  • Generated Clips Library: Saved locally for playback and sharing
  • App Settings: Your preferences and subscription status
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not backup your voice data to any cloud service

Temporary Backend Processing

  • Duration: Audio exists on our servers for 5-15 seconds during generation only
  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • Automatic Deletion: Files are immediately deleted after generation completes
  • No Logs: We do not log or store audio content from generations
  • Secure Infrastructure: Backend runs on Google Cloud Platform with enterprise security

Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Secure API authentication to prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Isolated processing - your data never mixes with other users
