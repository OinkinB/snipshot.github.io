How do I get better at the game?
Watch for yellow warnings before obstacles appear! Perfect runs (no deaths) earn 2√ó score multiplier. Speed matters too - finish levels under 15 seconds for 1.5√ó bonus. Stack both for 3√ó multiplier!
What's the highest score possible?
A perfect game (all levels completed without deaths or continues, with perfect timing) can score approximately 100,000-110,000 points. Can you beat it?
Can I remove ads?
Yes! The "Remove Ads" option will be available in future updates. For now, you can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Tracking.
I lost my progress! Can you restore it?
Unfortunately, no. Since all data is stored locally on your device (for privacy), if you delete the app or reset your phone, your scores are gone forever. This is the tradeoff for not requiring accounts or cloud storage.
Why are continues so expensive (2,000 points)?
Alien Space Lasers is designed to reward skillful play! The hefty continue penalty encourages players to improve and attempt perfect runs. It keeps the leaderboard meaningful.
Will you add more levels?
Maybe! The game is designed as a complete 21-level experience you can WIN. Future updates may add new game modes, challenges, or bonus planets based on player feedback. Send me your ideas!
Can I play offline?
Yes! The core game works completely offline. You only need internet for ads. No ads = no internet required.
