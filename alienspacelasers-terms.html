Game Rules and Mechanics

Fair Play Policy

Alien Space Lasers is designed to be challenging but fair. We implement several features to ensure fair gameplay:

  • Foreshadowing: Yellow warnings appear before obstacles spawn
  • Time Limits: 30-35 seconds per level to create urgency
  • Score Penalties: Deaths cost 500 points, continues cost 2,000 points
  • Skill-Based Multipliers: Perfect runs (no deaths) and fast completion earn 2-3√ó bonuses

Scoring System

Understanding the scoring system:

  • Target Collected: +100 points
  • Level Complete: +500 points (with multipliers for perfect/fast runs)
  • Boss Complete: +2,000 points
  • Time Bonus: +50 points per second remaining
  • Death Penalty: -500 points
  • Continue Penalty: -2,000 points

Leaderboard Rules

  • Top 20 game sessions are stored locally on your device
  • Sorted by highest score (victories and non-victories ranked together)
  • Displays: Score, Level, Planets Visited, Continue Count, Victory Status
  • No online leaderboard or multiplayer component
